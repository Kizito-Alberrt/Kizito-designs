Barefoot Project

The barefoot project is a website created with a group of developers with an aim of creating awareness to reach out to the vulnerable persons and children.

The aim of the website was to reach out to well wishers to support children to go to school and facilitate any other essentials that will aid their learning

Basically, it is a Community Based Organization that was started in the year 2020

Taking part in the starting of this website was a truly enlightening moment that opened doors to many young children who go to school barefoot and lack books and other stationeries.

The website includes a number of languages that were used, they include:

  • HTML
  • JavaScript
  • css
